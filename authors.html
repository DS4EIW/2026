DS4EIW 2026 - Instructions for Authors

Submission Guidelines

DS4EIW 2026 welcomes submissions presenting original results and substantial new work not currently under review or published elsewhere. The workshop focuses on data science solutions addressing equality, inclusion, and well-being challenges. The full list of topics of interest can be found on the Topics of Interest page.

Submission Format

Papers should be formatted to IEEE Computer Society Proceedings Manuscript Formatting Guidelines. Please refer to the formatting instructions here.

Please submit a full-length paper (from 5 pages up to 10 pages IEEE 2-column format) through the Online submission system.

Templates: IEEE provides templates in LaTeX and Word.

The submission link will be available soon. Please check back for updates.

Review Process

All submissions will be peer-reviewed by at least three members of the program committee. The review process will be single-blind.

Camera-Ready and Presentation Instructions

All final camera-ready papers must be uploaded before the deadline - March 9, 2026. These papers must adhere to the IEEE Computer Society Proceedings Manuscript Formatting Guidelines.

At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the workshop. Papers that are not presented at the workshop will not be included in the proceedings.

More information on the submission process will be provided closer to the deadline.

Publication

The workshop proceedings will be published by the IEEE Computer Society.

Presentation Format

The workshop will be held in online format. Authors of accepted papers will be notified of specific presentation requirements and details closer to the workshop date.
